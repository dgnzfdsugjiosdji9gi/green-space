About Our Project

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a park that is inclusive, environmentally friendly, and well-maintained. We aim to inspire the community to take part in protecting nature, keeping the park clean, and supporting sustainable urban spaces that everyone can enjoy.

Purpose

The purpose of our project is to help revitalize our local park while promoting environmental conservation. We aim to make the park cleaner, safer, and more enjoyable through awareness campaigns, proper waste sorting, community involvement, and responsible park use.
